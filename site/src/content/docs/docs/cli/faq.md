Q: What is the minimum config?
A: Configure either `api.host_token` and `api.base_url`, `notify.slack.webhook_url`, or `notify.discord.webhook_url`.

Q: Where do I get a host token?
A: From the dashboard, after creating a workspace and a host.

Q: Do I need both a host token and a webhook?
A: No. Any one of the supported delivery paths is enough.
