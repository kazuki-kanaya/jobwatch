---

Q: What is the minimum config?
A: Configure either `api.host_token` and `api.base_url`, or `notify.slack.webhook_url`.

Q: Where do I get a host token?
A: From the dashboard, after creating a workspace and a host.

Q: Do I need both token and webhook?
A: No. At least one is required.
